DeepScatter
Use deep learning to visualize similarity of images in a scatter plot.
This open source web-app lets you compare popular machine learning models and manifold learning algorithms with your own images. Generate scatter plots with similar images are placed close together and dissimilar images far apart. Find similarities, trends, differences, and outliers in your image set. While this is not a classification tool, you can apply colored labels to indiviual images at the time of uploading. Lastly, you can download hidden layer feature vectors to CSV files and do your own analysis.
